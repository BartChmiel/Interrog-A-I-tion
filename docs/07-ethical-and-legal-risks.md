# Ethical and Legal Risks

## Main Risk Areas

- Overreliance on AI-generated suggestions.
- Misinterpretation of advisory indicators as truthfulness scores.
- Hidden influence on official records.
- Processing personal or sensitive data without adequate safeguards.
- Generating leading, coercive, or manipulative questions.
- Poor explainability of model outputs.
- Weak audit trail for AI-assisted decisions.

## Controls

- Human authority over every AI suggestion.
- No automated guilt or truthfulness verdict.
- Source-linked grounding context.
- Citation validation.
- Prompt/context/output artifact capture.
- Append-only audit chain.
- Export integrity records.
- Synthetic-only repository data.
- Explicit security gates before non-synthetic material.

## Legal Position

The prototype is a research and engineering demonstrator. It is not an operational law-enforcement system and must not be presented as legally validated.

Future deployment would require jurisdiction-specific review, including data protection, procurement, evidentiary, labor, and AI governance requirements.

## Communication Standard

Product language must avoid claims such as:

- "detects lies",
- "proves credibility",
- "determines guilt",
- "automates interrogation",
- "replaces the interviewer".

Use "advisory", "source-linked", "operator-reviewed", and "working indicator" when describing AI-assisted outputs.
