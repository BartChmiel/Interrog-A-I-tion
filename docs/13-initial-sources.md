# Initial Sources

## Purpose

This document tracks starting points for legal, methodological, and technical research. It is not a final bibliography.

## Source Categories

- Investigative interviewing methodology.
- Forensic computing and digital evidence integrity.
- AI governance and high-risk AI systems.
- Data protection and anonymization or pseudonymization.
- Local AI deployment and model provenance.
- Human-computer interaction for professional tools.

## Research Notes

Sources used for thesis work should be separated into:

- normative/legal framework,
- methodology and psychology,
- technical architecture,
- evaluation design,
- product safety and governance.

## Documentation Rule

Claims about law, police procedure, psychology, or model safety should cite stable primary or high-quality institutional sources before being used in final thesis text or public-facing documentation.
